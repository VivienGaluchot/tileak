Play

Tileak is a tour based, multiplayer strategy game.
Your goal is to control all the board and get rid of the opponents.

It's still a proof of concept for serverless multiplaying,
and you may encounter issues according to your network setup.

1. Paste the joined peer's TAG and press "done"

2. Send your TAG back to the peer
